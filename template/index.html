My Projects

Trustify

In this project, I trained and compared seven different classification models:

Support Vector Machine (SVM), Random Forest, Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, XGBoost, LightGBM, and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN).

These models were evaluated based on multiple performance metrics (e.g., Accuracy, F1-score, AUC-ROC), and the best-performing model was used to classify incoming news articles.

Trustify allows users to input a news title and content, and it provides a trust score or percentage indicating the likelihood that the news is real or fake. This tool is intended to raise awareness and reduce the spread of misinformation by making AI-based news validation accessible to the public.

Study Buddy

Study Buddy is a personal project I developed during my leisure time to help students manage their study schedule effectively. The platform allows students to organize their subjects, topics, study sessions, and track progress while being reminded of their upcoming final exam dates. The goal of Study Buddy is to promote better time management and reduce last-minute cramming by encouraging consistent study habits. Users can plan study dates for each topic, monitor their completion status, and stay focused with timely reminders. This project reflects my interest in student-centered productivity tools and showcases my skills in web development, database design, and user experience planning. Future updates are planned to enhance features such as personalized study suggestions, performance analytics, and cross-device reminders.

My VR Gallery

My VR Gallery is a database-driven e-commerce web application developed as part of an academic assignment. The platform is designed specifically for admin and staff users to manage virtual product listings, inventory, and transactions in a streamlined and organized manner. The system allows admins to perform tasks such as: Adding, updating, and removing products Managing product categories and stock levels Monitoring sales and inventory data Handling user roles and access levels This project focused on applying database design principles, CRUD operations, and user role management within a secure and efficient platform. The backend is structured to ensure data integrity and support future scalability. My VR Gallery demonstrates my understanding of relational databases, web application logic, and role-based access control, and is a foundation for more advanced e-commerce systems.

UKM CGPA Calculator

UKM CGPA Calculator is a simple web application designed to help students calculate and predict their CGPA for better academic planning. By entering their grades and credit hours, students can instantly see their current CGPA and estimate future outcomes based on planned courses. This tool aims to support students in making informed decisions about their studies and achieving their academic goals.
