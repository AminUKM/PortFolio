STEM Workshop

I participated as a helper for the STEM Workshop conducted by Jazro at Cyberjaya for one day program, assisting tutors in conducting hands-on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics activities for students. My responsibilities included guiding participants, and ensuring a smooth and engaging learning experience. This role enhanced my communication and teamwork skills while inspiring students to explore STEM fields. These students were learning about MBOT, a robot that can be programmed to perform various tasks, and I assisted them in understanding the basics of programming and robotics.
